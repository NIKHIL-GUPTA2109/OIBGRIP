Tribute to Divine

Biography

Vivian Fernandes (born 2 October 1990), better known by his stage name Divine, is an Indian rapper from Mumbai. He started his career as an underground rapper in 2011 and gained popularity with his song "Yeh Mera Bombay". His breakthrough came in 2015 with the release of the single "Mere Gully Mein", which was later used as the soundtrack for the Bollywood film Gully Boy (2019).

Divine's music is known for its raw and gritty style, which reflects the harsh realities of life in the slums of Mumbai. His lyrics often touch upon social and political issues, and he is considered a leading voice of the Indian hip hop scene.
